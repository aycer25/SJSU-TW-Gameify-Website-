Overview

The SJSU Tech Writing Quest app helps students at San Jose State University keep track of their progress toward their Professional and Technical Writing (PTW) degree. The students can earn achievements and points in the app by completing certain classes, lessons, and workshops. This app will also connect students within the PTW program to network each other and do so much more!

Earn Achievements

What does the app do?

Our tech writing app simplifies the hassle of student life. The app keeps track of courses and academic requirements as well as extracurricular activities. It automatically links to canvas and mySJSU, so PTW students can access all their school information on one app.

Features

Personalized Avatar: Create your own avatar! Customize their eye color, hair color, and accessories.

Earn Achievements and Points: Win rewards by completing lessons that enhance your skills as a tech writer such as HTML, InDesign, and Adobe XD.

Point Shop: Spend your points customizing your avatar with new accessories!

Leaderboard: Compare your progress with other students in the PTW program. You can see what types of achievements others have accomplished as well as network with them through the app's messaging system.
